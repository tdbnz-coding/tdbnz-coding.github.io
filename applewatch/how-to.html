How to use these watch faces

Here is a simple guide for saving images and using them as custom Apple Watch faces.

1. Pick and download a face

  • Go to the Watch Faces page.
  • Click a face to open the large preview.
  • Select Download image.
  • On iPhone, choose Add to Photos or Save Image.

2. Set as a watch face from your iPhone

  1. Open the Photos app and find the downloaded image.
  2. Tap the Share button.
  3. Choose Create Watch Face.
  4. Pick Photos Watch Face or Kaleidoscope depending on the style.
  5. Adjust the time position and complications if available, then tap Add.

3. Set as a watch face from your watch

  1. Make sure the image is in your synced photo album for Apple Watch.
  2. On your watch, press firmly on the current watch face.
  3. Swipe until you see the photo based face you created.
  4. Tap to select it as your active face.

Tips for best results

  • Prefer high resolution images that match your watch screen ratio as closely as possible.
  • Avoid covering important parts of the image with the time or complications.
  • Try both light and dark faces to see what is easiest to read in bright light.
  • Create several faces and switch between them for work, fitness, and evenings.
