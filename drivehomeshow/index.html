The show

The sound of the drive home in Ōtautahi.

The Drive Home Show is your mid‑week reset from Christchurch and beyond—live every Wednesday on Plains Media 96.9 FM. Thomas and Jordan bring together music, local stories, interviews, and real community voices so the city doesn’t just commute—it connects.

Show details

  • Where: Plains Media 96.9 FM
  • Online: Stream via Plains FM
