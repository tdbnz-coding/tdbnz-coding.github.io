Wise Debit Card Review in New Zealand

Overview of the Wise Debit Card

The Wise Debit Card is a popular choice for overseas spending, offering low fees, mid-market exchange rates, and multi-currency capabilities. If you frequently travel or make online purchases in foreign currencies, signing up for a Wise Debit Card can help you save significantly compared to traditional bank cards.

ATM Fees and Currency Conversion

Wise offers **free ATM withdrawals** up to NZ$350 per month. Beyond this limit, a 1.75% fee applies, along with a NZ$1.50 per withdrawal charge. This is much cheaper than most bank cards, which charge higher fees for foreign withdrawals.

Pros and Cons of the Wise Debit Card

Pros

  • Low foreign transaction fees (0.49%)
  • Mid-market exchange rates with no hidden markup
  • No annual fees – only a one-off $14 issuance fee
  • Free ATM withdrawals up to NZ$350 per month
  • Integration with Google Pay

Cons

  • Not a credit card – funds must be preloaded
  • Replacement cards take 2-3 weeks to arrive
  • Free withdrawals are capped at NZ$350 per month
