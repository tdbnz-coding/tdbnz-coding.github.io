Trade Me Review

What is Trade Me?

Trade Me is New Zealand’s largest online marketplace, where users can buy, sell, and auction items. Since its launch in 1999, it has grown to become a hub for second-hand goods, property listings, cars, and job postings. With millions of active users, it is often considered New Zealand’s equivalent of eBay, but with a much broader reach into real estate, motor vehicles, and employment.

Categories on Trade Me

Trade Me offers a wide variety of categories:

  • General Marketplace: Buy and sell items such as clothing, electronics, and furniture.
  • Motors: A marketplace for cars, boats, and motorbikes.
  • Property: Browse and list homes for sale or rent.
  • Jobs: Search job listings and apply for employment directly on the platform.
  • Services: Hire professionals such as plumbers, cleaners, and more.

Trade Me Fees and Costs

Listing items on Trade Me is free for most categories, but there are fees for successful sales. The fee structure is outlined below:

Category Listing Fee Success Fee
General Items Free 7.9% of sale price (capped at $249)
Cars & Motors $39 for basic listings No success fee
Property Rentals $69 per listing No success fee

Trade Me vs Other Marketplaces

Trade Me faces competition from platforms like Facebook Marketplace, which offers free listings. However, Trade Me stands out with its buyer and seller protections, a wider variety of categories, and a trusted reputation.

Feature Trade Me Facebook Marketplace
Listing Fees Varies by category Free
Buyer Protection Yes No
Variety of Categories Extensive Limited

Pros and Cons of Trade Me

Pros

  • Wide variety of products and services.
  • User-friendly platform with a mobile app.
  • Trusted reputation with buyer and seller protections.
  • Dedicated categories for jobs, motors, and property.

Cons

  • Success fees can be high for expensive items.
  • Competition from free alternatives like Facebook Marketplace.
  • Some users report slow customer service responses.

Conclusion: Is Trade Me Worth It?

Trade Me remains the go-to platform for New Zealanders looking to buy, sell, or trade. While there are free alternatives, Trade Me offers greater security and a broader range of categories. For anyone serious about selling cars, property, or services, Trade Me is the better option.
