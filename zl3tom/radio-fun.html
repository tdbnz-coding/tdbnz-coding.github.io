International Radio Network

The International Radio Network’s mission is to promote the amateur radio hobby to those interested in radio communications. We welcome licensed and non-licensed operators, giving those with an interest the opportunity to gain experience in radio communications, theory, technology, and fellowship, and hopefully some fun along the way.

IRN can be accessed by Teamspeak, Zello, DMR, AllStar, System Fusion, and EchoLink.

ZMR Radio

Network radio and ZMR over Zello is another mode of two-way radio, not to replace ham radio, but to compliment it. Just as DMR relies on internet connectivity for linking, so does ZMR and Network Radio, same idea as Fusion, DStar, EchoLink, AllStar, IRLP, and others.

Network Radios

Love radio, want to meet new people and have fun. Check out the Network Radios channels, they have a range of channels on Zello for licensed and unlicensed users, they cater for everyone. They have a shop dedicated to Network Radios too.
