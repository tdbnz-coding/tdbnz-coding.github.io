QSL

QSL and confirmations

QRZ Logbook, easiest way

I would love to log you on QRZ. If you log the QSO on QRZ and send me the details too, I can match it quickly and confirm. I will do the same on my side, this way it is easy for both of us.

Send me this info for each QSO
  • Your callsign
  • Date and time in UTC
  • Band or frequency, or the network name if it was EchoLink or AllStar
  • Mode, for example SSB, FM, digital, EchoLink, AllStar
  • Signal report if you have it, not required but helpful
Copy and paste template
Callsign: YOURCALL
Contacted: ZL3TOM (or ZL3KY)
UTC date: YYYY-MM-DD
UTC time: HH:MM
Band/Freq or Network: 40m 7.090, or EchoLink 304602, or AllStar 40452
Mode: SSB / FM / Digital / EchoLink / AllStar
RST: 59 / 599 (optional)
