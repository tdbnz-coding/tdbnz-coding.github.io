QSL

QSL and confirmations

Fastest option, QRZ Logbook

If you use QRZ Logbook, that is usually the quickest way for confirmation. Make sure your callsign is logged correctly, including mode and date.

Email

Send details to:

zl3tom@tdbnz.email

What to include

  • Your callsign
  • Date and time in UTC
  • Band or frequency, or the network name if it was EchoLink or AllStar
  • Mode, for example SSB, FM, digital, EchoLink, AllStar
  • Signal report if you have it, not required but helpful

Network QSOs

Include the node, plus UTC time.
