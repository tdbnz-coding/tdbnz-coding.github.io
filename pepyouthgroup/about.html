Welcome to Pep Youth Group - Disability Youth Group!

 

At Pep Youth Group, we believe in creating a supportive and inclusive environment where young people with disabilities can come together, connect, and thrive. Our mission is simple: to empower and inspire every member to reach their full potential while fostering a sense of belonging and community.

Founded with the vision of providing a safe space for young individuals with disabilities to socialize, learn, and grow, Pep Youth Group has been a beacon of positivity and encouragement in the community since its inception. Our dedicated team of volunteers and staff are committed to ensuring that every member feels valued, respected, and supported on their journey.

Located conveniently at the Linwood Library at Eastgate, our weekly meetings take place every Thursday from 5pm to 7pm during the school term. This central location allows easy access for our members and their families, fostering a sense of community within the heart of our city.

But Pep Youth Group is more than just a meeting place. It's a vibrant hub of activity where friendships are forged, skills are developed, and dreams are nurtured. From engaging workshops and creative projects to fun outings and special events, there's always something exciting happening at Pep.

We understand that every individual is unique, which is why we offer a variety of activities and programs tailored to meet the diverse interests and needs of our members. Whether it's art and crafts, sports and recreation, or life skills and personal development, there's something for everyone at Pep Youth Group.

But perhaps the most important aspect of Pep is the sense of belonging and acceptance that permeates everything we do. Here, differences are celebrated, and everyone is encouraged to embrace their true selves without fear of judgment or discrimination. It's a place where laughter fills the air, where challenges are faced head-on, and where dreams take flight.

So, if you're looking for a place to belong, grow, and thrive, look no further than Pep Youth Group - Disability Youth Group. Join us on this incredible journey of friendship, empowerment, and possibility. Together, we can make a difference, one smile at a time.
